About the Company

How we got started:

The OzarksMade company originated under a different name and on a different website. Our main service was a blog hosted on Google's BlogSpot under the name 'Ozarks Tech Thoughts'. We also offered a few random Android Apps created using the Sketchware IDE. You can still download those today here on the OzarksMade Apps page, however they are no longer supported.

How we have progressed:

Slowly we have grown to become what we are today. As we began to offer more products and services, our website grew and became more robust as we transitioned into the 'OzarksMade' Company.

How we got our name:

It seems like most things these days are Made in China and if they aren't, they mind as well be. Most companies these days that offer products and services related to tech have headquarters in Southern California. Products and Services made by The OzarksMade Company are only Made in the Ozarks, hence the name 'OzarksMade'.

About our Developer

Where he's located:

OzarksMade's Developer, Richard Browning, is located in the Ozarks Mountains in Southwest Missouri, hence why all of the products and services we offer are made only right here, in the Ozarks.

Who Richard is:

Richard Browning, OzarksMade Owner and Developer, is simply a person who loves programming and development, and simply wanted to develop something in his free time that everyone involved could benefit from. You can contact Richard by contacting the OzarksMade Company here on the OzarksMade Contact page.

How Richard started developing:

Richard wasn't always as interested or knowledgeable in Coding and Development as he now is. It was basically by chance that he became as interested as he now is, and that he learned as much as he did in the field of mobile development. HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Node.js are the programming languages Richard is most knowledgeable in. The Android Apps OzarksMade offers have been primarily created with the Sketchware IDE.
